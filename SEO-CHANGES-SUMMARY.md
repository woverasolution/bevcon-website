# BevCon Website — SEO Keyword Integration

## What changed and why

The BevCon website previously had no mentions of key industry terms that potential clients and investors search for — specifically **EPC**, **EPCM**, and **turnkey** — and no reference to the geographic regions BevCon operates in. This update weaves those terms naturally throughout the site so that search engines (and AI-powered search tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.) can surface BevCon for relevant queries.

## Target search terms

- Turnkey EPC Brewery
- Brewery EPC Contractor
- EPCM Beverage Plant Engineering
- German Brewery and Beverage Engineering
- Searches combining these terms with **Africa**, **Asia**, and **the Caribbean**

## What was updated

### Page title and search result preview
The browser tab and Google search result now reads:
**"BevCon – German Brewery & Beverage EPC Engineering | Turnkey Projects Worldwide"**
instead of the previous generic company name. The description shown beneath the title in search results now includes EPC, EPCM, turnkey, and the three target regions.

### Hero section (top of the homepage)
- The badge above the headline now says **"German Beverage & Brewery Engineering"**
- The introductory paragraph now positions BevCon as an **independent EPC and EPCM engineering partner** delivering projects across **Africa, Asia, and the Caribbean**
- "Precision engineering" updated to **"German precision engineering"**

### "What We Do" overview
- The introductory sentence now mentions **turnkey EPC and EPCM** and **brewery**
- The Construction card now references **Turnkey EPC** project execution
- The Contracts card now references **EPC and EPCM contracts**

### Beverage Construction section
- The subheading now leads with **"Turnkey EPC Delivery"**
- The description now includes **turnkey brewery and beverage plant**, **EPCM engineering partner**, **German engineering discipline**, and the three geographic regions

### Beverage Contracts section
- The FIDIC description now specifies **turnkey EPC brewery and beverage plant projects**
- The Silver Book role description now references **EPC brewery** projects

### Beverage Concept section
- The Market Strategy description now names **Africa, Asia, and the Caribbean** instead of the vague "your geography"

### Call-to-action section
- The headline now says **"brewery or beverage plant project"** instead of just "beverage project"
- The description now positions BevCon as a **German EPC and EPCM engineering partner** with the geographic regions and **turnkey** included

### Footer
- The brand tagline now reads: **"German EPC and EPCM engineering partner delivering turnkey brewery and beverage plant projects across Africa, Asia, and the Caribbean."**

### Behind-the-scenes (structured data)
- Added detailed structured data (JSON-LD) that tells Google and AI search tools exactly what BevCon does, where it operates, and answers three common industry questions — this helps with featured snippets and AI-generated answers
- Added a proper `robots.txt` file that guides search engine crawlers

## What was NOT changed
- The main headline ("From Concept to First Bottle / Market Leader / ...") remains untouched
- The visual design, layout, colors, and animations are all unchanged
- No new pages were added — all changes are within existing content
